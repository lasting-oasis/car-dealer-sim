Engagement & Monetization

A game inside the sim.

🏁

Drive what you own — or rent

Race your own inventory, or rent a car at the speedway. All driving burns fuel; gas stations and a fuel economy keep the loop honest.

Three difficulty tiers with progressively faster AI. Place 1st for the full purse — bigger rewards, harder rivals. A clean risk/reward gradient.

Under the Hood

Built lean. Built to scale.

A custom real-time 3D engine — no bloated game framework — that loads instantly in any browser.

🧊

Custom WebGL Engine

A hand-written Three.js scene — instanced rendering, custom driving physics, day/night, and a logarithmic depth buffer for a world that spans kilometers.

⚡

Real-Time & Server-Authoritative

socket.io keeps every player in one coherent, cheat-resistant world, with throttled position sync and a type-checked, gated build pipeline.

🧩

React · TypeScript · Zustand

A modern, maintainable client. Strict type-checking now gates the build, so the codebase stays healthy as the team grows.

☁️

Zero-Install, Cross-Platform

One URL. Desktop and mobile, no download, no app store. Frictionless distribution and instant top-of-funnel.
